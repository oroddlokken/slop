# Challenge Me

Quick sanity check on your current approach. Spawns a fast Sonnet agent to challenge whether you're solving the right problem the right way.

Use after building something, before committing to a direction, or when something feels off.

## What you get

A single-round gut-check that evaluates:

- **Approach fit** — is this the right solution shape?
- **Blind spots** — what breaks at 2 AM?
- **Tradeoffs** — what are you silently trading away?
- **Verdict** — GOOD, TWEAK, or RETHINK

## Installation

Tell your agent to read this repository and ask it to help you integrate it into your Claude Code setup as a skill.
